About Me

At present, I am working as an Associate Professor at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), Manipal. I worked as a SERB-TARE research fellow at the Spectrum Lab with Prof. Chandra Sekhar Seelamantula, Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore during 2019-2022. I am a cofounder of Infyuva Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a startup company, currently incubated at MUTBI, MAHE, Manipal.
